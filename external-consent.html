Participate in a Cognitive Science experiment

Language processing

Please do not participate if you are a minor

You will answered a few demographic questions, followed by an experiment in cognitive science.

The experiment lasts around 10 minutes. Please take it in one go, alone, in a quiet setting, while remaining focused. Your efforts are crucial for the collected data to be interpretable. Your participation is free and you can stop at any time, simply by quitting this page. If you stop before the end, the data will not be collected or used.

Only your answers and response times will be collected and analysed. The results from this experiment might be used in scientific publications or presentations. There are no risks of any kind in this study. You are welcome to asks questions about this research project at any time by contacting the experimenters.
